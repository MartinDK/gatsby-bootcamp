> Good politics shouldn't be tribal, it should be tribe building.

Everyone wants to be part of a tribe with shared values. People avoid being isolated and will adapt to fit the tribe. In a tribe members conform to shared goals and values. When tribes work together they can form a Nation. Successful nations are able to integrate tribes into a cohesive society.

- Tribes within a nation compete or work with each other.
- Competition between tribes in a functioning *nation* don't lead to conflict.
- Healthy competition promotes peaceful and merit based hierarchy.
- Unhealthy competition leads to violent conflicts.

Not all relationships are tribal. Groups provide connect people with shared interest.

- Religion provides the role of connecting people with shared values.  
- Tribes connect people with shared cultures/location.
- Social groups are the people whom we spend time with, and have *personal relationships*.

### Modern Tribes

The workplace/business is where tribes are formed in modern society. A stable workplace builds a strong tribe. When employment is unstable it weakens the tribe. Society is made up of many workplaces/tribes.

### How do tribes choose their leader?

The leader of tribe is the person in charge of the workplace. Employers have a big effect on employee's personal life.

- Free time
- Social group
- Status
- Income
