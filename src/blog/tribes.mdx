> Good politics shouldn't be tribal.

People want to be part of a tribe with shared values. To avoid being isolated they will adapt to fit the tribe. In a tribe people conform to shared goals and values. When tribes work together they form a Nation. Successful nations will integrate tribes into a cohesive nation.

- Tribes within a nation compete or work with each other.
- Competition of tribes within a nation don't lead to conflict.
- Healthy competition leads peaceful/merit hierarchy and status.
- Unhealthy competition leads to conflict.

### Questions

- How do rewards shape the tribe?
  - defines value
  - defines status
- How do rewards shape people's identity in the tribe?

 

Not all relationships are tribal. Groups provide connect people with shared interest.

- Religion provides the role of connecting people with shared values.  
- Tribes connect people with shared cultures/location.
- Social groups are the people whom we spend time with, and have *personal relationships*.

### Modern Tribes

The workplace forms the tribe in modern society. A stable workplace builds a strong tribe. When employment is unstable it weakens the tribe. Society is made up of many workplace/tribes.

### How do tribes choose their leader?

The leader of tribe is the person in charge of the workplace. Employers have a big effect on employee's personal life.

- Free time
- Social group
- Status
- Income
